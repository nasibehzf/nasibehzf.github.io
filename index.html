Nasibeh Zanjirani Farahani, PhD, CSM

Translational AI Leader | Assistant Professor of Medicine | Mayo Clinic Platform

Overview

Dr. Nasibeh Zanjirani Farahani is a translational AI leader and systems engineer focused on advancing high-impact, trustworthy artificial intelligence in healthcare. Her work bridges clinical needs, emerging technologies, and strategic implementation to deliver scalable solutions that improve patient outcomes, operational performance, and health equity.

Leadership & Impact

At Mayo Clinic Platform, she leads cross-sector collaborations with startups, researchers, and industry partners to design, evaluate, and deploy AI solutions in real-world clinical environments. She has mentored dozens of AI companies and contributed to the development of responsible AI frameworks and national initiatives shaping the future of healthcare innovation.

Focus Areas

Translational AI • Clinical Decision Support • Health Systems Engineering • Responsible AI • Health Equity • Machine Learning in Practice • AI Strategy & Deployment

Selected Work

Her work spans clinical AI applications, startup acceleration, and research initiatives focused on integrating machine learning into healthcare workflows, including risk stratification models, real-world validation, and equitable data design.
